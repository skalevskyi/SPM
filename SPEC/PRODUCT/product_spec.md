# Product Specification — SPM

## Product Concept

SPM is a **micro-OOH (out-of-home) advertising** service: **one vehicle, one route, three formats.** A single branded vehicle (Nissan Qashqai SUV) circulates daily on a fixed route in Montpellier and the Littoral. Advertisers buy space on the vehicle (rear, sides, or full wrap) and benefit from estimated visibility linked to the route and methodology. **Local visibility for Montpellier and the Littoral.**

## Vehicle

- **Model:** Nissan Qashqai SUV.
- **Use:** Unique mobile media support; one vehicle ensures consistent route and measurable exposure.
- **Visibility:** Depends on format (Rear / Side / Full) and route exposure. See visibility_model.md.

## Coverage Area

Daily circulation between (curated place names per locale; see website_spec.md or DESIGN_SYSTEM.md — Localization):

- **Montpellier**
- **Port Marianne**
- **Carnon**
- **Palavas**
- **La Grande-Motte**

The route is defined and repeated to allow visibility modelling (see visibility_model.md).

## Advertising Formats

**Primary names:** **Rear**, **Side**, **Full.**

Legacy mapping: Rear (formerly BASIC), Side (formerly PRO), Full (formerly EXCLUSIVE).

| Format | Description              | Positioning        |
| ------ | ------------------------ | ------------------ |
| **Rear**  | Rear advertising         | Rear of vehicle    |
| **Side**  | Side advertising         | Sides of vehicle   |
| **Full**  | Full vehicle branding   | Rear + sides + wrap|

Each format has associated visibility estimates (indicative placeholders; see visibility_model.md) and pricing determined by an estimation model (see pricing_model.md; no fixed prices on the website in Phase 2A).

## Format Compatibility Rules

- **Rear and Side:** May coexist with another advertiser on the vehicle if there is no direct competitive conflict (e.g. different vehicle zones, non-competing sectors).
- **Full:** **Exclusive format.** Only one advertiser occupies the entire vehicle; no coexistence with other brands on the same vehicle.

## Unique Value Proposition

- **Local focus:** Concentrated visibility in Montpellier and the Littoral, ideal for local businesses.
- **Mobile medium:** Message moves with traffic and parking, unlike static billboards.
- **Clear formats:** Three levels (Rear, Side, Full) with transparent visibility ranges and dynamic pricing (no fixed price ladder on the site in current phase).
- **B2B-oriented:** Designed for restaurants, agencies, garages, salons, events and local services.

## Visibility Summary (Indicative Placeholders)

- **Rear:** ~5 000 contacts/day, ~110 000/month.
- **Side:** ~7 000 contacts/day, ~150 000/month.
- **Full:** ~9 000 contacts/day, ~200 000/month.

Methodology and assumptions are described in visibility_model.md. These figures are indicative and will be refined using the methodology and real route/traffic data where available.
