## Google Business Optimization

- **Profile:** SPM — publicité mobile Montpellier / Littoral.
- **Category:** Advertising agency or relevant local category.
- **Zone:** Montpellier and/or main cities on the route.
- **Description:** Value proposition, formats (Rear, Side, Full), coverage (Montpellier, Port Marianne, Carnon, Palavas, La Grande-Motte), indicative pricing/calculator mention, CTA.
- **Photos:** Vehicle (rear, sides, full wrap), route schematic, optional client or sector examples.
- **Posts:** Offers, new formats, blog links, visibility figures (indicative), testimonials when available.
- **Reviews:** Encourage B2B reviews (Google, optional other platforms); respond professionally.
- **Q&A:** Pre-fill frequent questions (how it works, pricing/estimation, visibility, contract).
