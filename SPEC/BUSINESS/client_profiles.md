# Client Profiles — SPM

Ideal client segments for mobile advertising in Montpellier and the Littoral.

## Restaurants

- **Need:** Visibility to locals and tourists (Carnon, Palavas, La Grande-Motte, Montpellier).
- **Use:** Promotions, terrace, new opening, delivery.
- **Formats:** Rear for smaller budgets; Side or Full for strong branding (e.g. beach/events).

## Real Estate Agencies

- **Need:** Recognition in the area; visibility to potential buyers and renters.
- **Use:** Agency branding, specific programmes or developments (e.g. Port Marianne).
- **Formats:** Side or Full for professional image and memorability.

## Garages

- **Need:** Local trust and visibility (drivers on the route).
- **Use:** Garage name, services, promotions (tyres, MOT, repairs).
- **Formats:** Rear to Side; Full for main partners or multi-site groups.

## Beauty Salons

- **Need:** Local clientele, especially in residential and commercial zones on the route.
- **Use:** Salon name, offers, new services, opening.
- **Formats:** Rear or Side; Full for flagship or franchise.

## Events

- **Need:** One-off or seasonal visibility (festivals, sports, cultural events in the area).
- **Use:** Event name, date, venue, ticket or registration.
- **Formats:** All three; choice according to budget and duration (e.g. Rear for short campaigns).

## Local Services

- **Need:** Plumbers, electricians, painters, movers, etc. seeking local B2C visibility.
- **Use:** Brand, phone number, website, “call now” message.
- **Formats:** Rear or Side; message simple and readable (contact details).
