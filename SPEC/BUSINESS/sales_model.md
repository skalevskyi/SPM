# Sales Model — ADMOVE

## Value Proposition

- **Local mobile visibility** in Montpellier and the Littoral at a contained budget.
- **Three clear formats** (Rear, Side, Full) with transparent visibility ranges and **dynamic pricing** via an estimation model (no fixed price ladder on the website in current phase).
- **Predictable exposure** via a fixed route and a documented visibility methodology (see visibility_model.md).
- **B2B-oriented:** Designed for local businesses that need simple, effective outdoor advertising.

## Core Sales Arguments

1. **Geographic focus:** The vehicle circulates where the client’s customers live and move (Montpellier, Port Marianne, Carnon, Palavas, La Grande-Motte).
2. **Visibility figures:** Daily and monthly contact estimates per format (indicative placeholders; see visibility_model.md) support comparison with other media (e.g. CPM when combined with quote).
3. **Flexible entry:** Rear allows testing without large investment; Side and Full for stronger impact. Pricing is determined by estimation (calculator in Phase 2B); no fixed prices displayed on the landing in Phase 2A.
4. **One medium, one route:** Easy to understand and explain; no complex media plan.
5. **Mobile vs static:** Message moves with traffic and parking, increasing touchpoints compared to a single billboard.

## Pricing Positioning

- **Rear:** Entry level, rear only — for testing or limited budget.
- **Side:** Sides included — better visibility/price ratio for regular campaigns.
- **Full:** Full branding — maximum impact and memorability; **exclusive** (one advertiser per vehicle).

Pricing is determined dynamically using the estimation model (see pricing_model.md). Positioning: affordable local outdoor, with clear steps from trial to full visibility. No fixed prices (250 € / 450 € / 750 €) on the website.

## Format Compatibility (sales context)

- Rear and Side may coexist with another advertiser on the vehicle if there is no direct competitive conflict (product_spec.md).
- Full is exclusive: one advertiser occupies the entire vehicle.

## Outreach Approach

- **Landing page:** Primary tool to capture leads (contact form, quote request). Structure: Hero, Le média mobile, Parcours, Offres, Contact, Footer (see website_spec.md).
- **Target segments:** Restaurants, real estate agencies, garages, beauty salons, events, local services (see client_profiles.md).
- **Channels:** SEO, Google Business, direct outreach (email/phone), local partnerships.
- **Content:** Use visibility and estimation logic from SPEC; media kit for serious prospects when available.
- **Follow-up:** Qualify leads by format interest and budget; propose Rear for first contact where relevant.
